What is Dine4Six?

We all lead busy lives and in the chaos we tend to get stuck in our comfort zone. Never straying too from our social circles and constantly attracting the same type of people. Dine4Six takes the apprehension and difficulty out of meeting new people. Dine4Six hosts events that allow you to meet new and interesting people over a casual dinner at some of your cities favourite restaurants events. Dine differently, Dine4Six.

  • Meet new people when travelling

    Dine4Six lets you meet new people in new cities. If you are a solo traveller looking to meet new people along your adventure, Dine4Six will always be your travel companion.

  • Network on your business trips

    Ever been on a business trip and wanted to try a new restaurant but didn't want to dine alone. Don't dine alone, Dine4Six.

  • Expand your social cicles

    You love you friends and your friends love you, but sometime you just need a change of pace and scenery. Dine4Six lets you dine with a diverse range of people who you would never normally meet. Why not try something new?

  • Access exclusive bookings

    Ever wanted to book a table at that exclusive restaurant but the waiting list is weeks, or even months, long? We do our bookings long in advance, all you have to do is book a seat.
